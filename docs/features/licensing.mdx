Sencho uses an open-core model. The **Community** tier is free forever with unlimited nodes. **Pro** unlocks advanced features like fleet management, RBAC, webhooks, atomic deployments, and fleet-wide backups.

## Plans

| Tier | Price | Accounts |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Community** | Free | 1 admin |
| **Personal Pro** | $7.99/month, $69.99/year, or $249 lifetime | 1 admin + 3 viewers |
| **Team Pro** | $49.99/month, $499.99/year, or $1,499 lifetime | Unlimited |

Lifetime pricing is an early-adopter offer available for a limited time only.

## Free trial

Every new Sencho installation starts with a **14-day Pro trial** — no license key or credit card required. All Pro features are unlocked during the trial so you can evaluate them with your real infrastructure.

When the trial expires, Sencho automatically reverts to the Community tier. No data is lost.

## Purchasing a license

There are two ways to subscribe:

### In-app checkout

1. Go to **Settings > License** in your Sencho dashboard.
2. Click **Personal — $69.99/yr** or **Team — $499.99/yr** (or visit [sencho.io/pricing](https://sencho.io/pricing) for all options).
3. Complete the checkout on Lemon Squeezy.
4. If your instance is reachable from the internet, the license activates automatically via webhook.
5. If not, you'll receive a license key by email — enter it in the activation field.

### Manual activation

If you purchased a license key separately:

1. Go to **Settings > License**.
2. Paste your key into the **Activate License Key** field.
3. Click **Activate**.

Sencho validates the key against Lemon Squeezy and activates Pro features immediately.

## License validation

Active licenses are re-validated every **72 hours** against the Lemon Squeezy API. If your instance goes offline, there is a **30-day grace period** before it degrades to the Community tier.

## Managing your subscription

If your license was purchased through checkout, a **Manage Subscription** button appears in Settings > License. This opens the Lemon Squeezy customer portal where you can:

- Update your payment method
- View invoices and receipts
- Cancel or resume your subscription
- Switch between plans

## Webhook integration

<Note>
  Webhook support is optional. It enables automatic license activation and real-time subscription updates.
</Note>

If your Sencho instance is publicly accessible, you can configure a Lemon Squeezy webhook to send events directly to it:

**Webhook URL:** `https://your-sencho-domain/api/webhooks/lemonsqueezy`

Sencho handles these events automatically:

| Event | Action |
|-------|--------|
| `order_created` | Activates license (lifetime purchases) |
| `order_refunded` | Revokes license |
| `subscription_created` | Activates license |
| `subscription_updated` | Updates plan/variant |
| `subscription_cancelled` | Keeps active until end of period |
| `subscription_expired` | Revokes license |
| `subscription_paused` | Pauses license |
| `subscription_resumed` | Reactivates license |
| `subscription_payment_failed` | Logs warning (grace period) |
| `subscription_payment_success` | Confirms renewal |

All webhook requests are verified using HMAC-SHA256 signatures before processing.

## Deactivating a license

To transfer your license to a different instance:

1. Go to **Settings > License**.
2. Click **Deactivate License**.
3. On your new instance, activate the same license key.

Deactivation reverts the current instance to the Community tier immediately.
